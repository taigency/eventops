## Step-by-step: creating the pages (you do this part)

### Facebook Page
1. facebook.com → Pages → **Create new Page**
2. Name: `EventOps` · Category: `Software company`
3. Add bio (short intro above), profile pic (`social-avatar`), cover (`og-cover`)
4. Add website `eventops.africa`, and a call-to-action button → "Sign up" → your app URL
5. Post the launch post (see launch-content.md)

### Instagram
1. Create account with your chosen handle → switch to **Professional → Business**
2. Category "Software", add bio, link (use eventops.africa or a Linktree)
3. Profile pic = `social-avatar`
4. Connect it to the Facebook Page (Meta Business Suite) so you post once, publish to both

### LinkedIn Company Page
1. linkedin.com → Work menu → **Create a Company Page** → Small business
2. Name `EventOps`, URL `linkedin.com/company/eventops` (or eventops-africa if taken)
3. Add logo (`social-avatar` or `logo-mark`), cover (`og-cover`), tagline + About
4. Publish, then invite your connections to follow

> Tip: set up **Meta Business Suite** (business.facebook.com) to manage the FB + IG pages
> and schedule posts from one place, free.
