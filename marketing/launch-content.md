# EventOps — Launch Content & Ads

Copy-paste ready. Written for South African event & production companies.

---

## The one-liner (use everywhere)
> **Run your events like clockwork.** The all-in-one ERP for event & production teams — quotes, crew, run sheets and live comms in one place.

## Elevator pitch (30 seconds)
> Event companies lose hours every week juggling spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups and paper run sheets — and things fall through the cracks. EventOps puts the whole operation in one system: quote a job, build the budget, schedule your crew, lay out the run sheet, and brief everyone live on event day. From first enquiry to load-out, one source of truth.

## Who it's for
Event management companies · production & AV houses · staging & rigging · conference organisers · catering & hospitality ops · brand-activation agencies.

## The problem → the fix
| Today | With EventOps |
|---|---|
| Quotes in Excel, re-typed every time | Professional quotes & budgets in minutes |
| Crew booked over WhatsApp | Crew scheduling with roles & responsibilities |
| Run sheets printed and outdated by call time | Live run sheets, every department, one timeline |
| "Who's doing what?" on the day | Real-time event-day comms to the whole team |
| Client info scattered across inboxes | Per-event client, brief & document management |

## 30-second promo video script (for the screen-recording)

Open `marketing/promo.html` full-screen and screen-record while it auto-plays, or use it as a
storyboard. Suggested voiceover / on-screen captions:

```
[0-4s]   "Running events is chaos. Spreadsheets. WhatsApp. Paper run sheets."
[4-9s]   "EventOps puts it all in one system."
[9-15s]  "Quote a job and build the budget in minutes."   (show quoting screen)
[15-21s] "Schedule your crew — every role, every shift."  (show crew screen)
[21-27s] "Build the run sheet. Brief the whole team live." (show run sheet + comms)
[27-30s] "EventOps. Run your events like clockwork. Start free at eventops.africa."
```

**How to record:** Windows `Win + Alt + R` (Xbox Game Bar) or the free OBS Studio. Record the
browser at 1920×1080, add the voiceover or captions in CapCut (free), export MP4. Post as an
IG Reel, FB video and LinkedIn native video.
