management stack

Built specifically for the complexity of live events — not adapted from generic project management tools.

🧾

Smart Quotation Builder

Compile vendor quotes by category, apply margin, management fee, VAT and discounts. Generate branded client proposals in one click.

💰

Finance & Milestone Invoicing

Triple-Match accounting (Scope → PO → Invoice), milestone-based invoicing, tax invoice generation and payment tracking.

👷

Workforce Marketplace

Post open positions to a public Crew Board. Freelance crew apply with their quote. Directors review, accept and track contracts.

💬

Comms Hub — Replace WhatsApp

Section group chats (Production, AV, Catering, Logistics…), direct messages and broadcast announcements. All in one place.

⚡

Ripple Engine

Simulate setup delays and watch costs cascade across labour, accommodation, catering and fuel in real time. Know your risk before it hits.

📋

Production Brief & Weather

Auto-populated production schedule with section contacts. Live weather forecast for the venue. Printable PDF brief for all crew.

🛡️

Supplier Compliance

Track vendor CSD registration, B-BBEE level and affidavit expiry dates. Automatic alerts for non-compliant suppliers.

📱

Field App for Crew

Mobile-optimised field view for technicians. Personal call sheet, duty confirmation, document uploads, and run sheet access.

⚙️

White-Label Documents

Upload your logo, choose colours and customise terms per document type. Every quote, invoice, PO and contract carries your brand.
